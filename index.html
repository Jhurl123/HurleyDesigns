Object Oriented

Database Design

Here we use mySQL to populate databases with given information to store and access at a later date. We perform queries on the data to search for information to meet the specific requirements that the user wishes to see. Allows complete access to only the data requested and not all of the data making it easier for non technical reports to management and clients. Demonstrates example usage of views and cursors.

Android SQL

This app is a database that allows you to store the information from a business card given to you, and keep the information stored locally on your phone. Made with sqlLite, this app offers the ability to edit data once it is entered. Very handy for people who accrue millions of business cards throughout their professional and personal life.
